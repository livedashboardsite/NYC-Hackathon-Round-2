## 🏆 **Problem Statement**
### CREATIVE-01 — Make Boring Impossible

> *"Design and build a product or experience that people genuinely enjoy using and naturally want to share with others."*

**TidyQuest answers this by turning the tedious chore of notification cleanup into a satisfying, shareable mini-game.** No login. No setup. Just pure, addictive fun.

## ✨ **Features**

<table align="center">
<tr>
<td width="50%">

### 🎯 **Satisfying Dismiss**
- Click, drag, or use keyboard
- Particles + sound + screen shake
- Every action feels GOOD

### 🔥 **Streak System**
- Fast dismissals = bigger combos
- Visual color shift as you heat up
- "Streak broken" warning

</td>
<td width="50%">

### 📊 **Real-Time Feedback**
- Live score counter
- Timer tracks your speed
- Progress ring shows remaining

### 📸 **Shareable Recap**
- "Tidier than X%" metric
- Download as PNG
- Tweet directly to Twitter
